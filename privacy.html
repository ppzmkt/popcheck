Privacy Policy

Auto Checkout Extension is designed to automate checkout flows on Pop Mart websites. This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, except for the user's email address if explicitly provided through extension functionality.

The email address is used solely to support features such as user tracking or personalized settings and is not shared with any third parties. The extension does not access or track browsing activity outside of specified Pop Mart domains.

We are committed to safeguarding user data and ensuring full compliance with Chrome Web Store policies.

隐私权政策（中文）

Auto Checkout Extension 插件用于在 Pop Mart 官网执行自动化结账流程。除非用户主动在插件中提供，本插件不会收集、存储或传输任何个人数据，唯一涉及的电子邮件地址仅用于支持个性化功能。

用户的电子邮件地址仅限于插件内部功能使用，绝不会共享给第三方。插件也不会访问 Pop Mart 以外的网站或跟踪您的浏览行为。

我们致力于保护用户数据，确保完全遵守 Chrome 扩展商店的隐私政策。
